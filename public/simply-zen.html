SIMPLY ZEN

DESCRIPTION

Learn to Meditate

Simply Zen is an elegant, content rich mindfulness app that was developed for people who want to learn how to mediate and enjoy the benefits that a mindfulness practice can bring.

I created Simply Zen: Guided Meditaion to make learning to meditate affordable to anyone with an iPhone. Most of the apps on the app store either didn't meet my needs, or charged a hundred dollars per year in subscription fees. Simply Zen doesn't work like that. You pay once, and it's yours for life.

Meditation Screen


Features of Simply Zen:

  • Animated Menus
  • Guided meditation courses
  • Guided meditations for your moods
  • Open ended meditations with bells to keep you present, or without any sound at all
  • Customizable bell sounds with five bells to choose from
  • HealthKit integration
  • Inspirational quotes at the end of your session
  • Awe inspiring images that are displayed with the quote
  • Twitter support that allows you to tweet out the quotes you love
  • Get back to your favorite place, or where you left off with 3D touch shortcuts

Upcoming and Planned Features:

  • Streaks to help keep you on track
  • Achievements
  • Tibetan Buddhist Guided Course
  • Relaxing background sounds and music that you can turn on while you meditate
  • Contact me to get your feature on the upcoming features list
