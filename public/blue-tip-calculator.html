BLUE TIP CALCULATOR

DESCRIPTION

Minimalist Tip Calculation

Blue Tip Calculator is a simple, elegant tip solution for the minimalist on the go. It supports iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

This started out as an assignment in one of the free online Swift courses I was working on. After finishing it, I figured I may as well submit it to the App Store. It was an exciting experience to see it get approved, and then to see it getting some downloads. Later on, when the Apple Watch was released, I wrote an extension so you could use it on the watch. Now, I think the watch version is much nicer, and slicker than the iOS version.

Blue Tip Calculator on iPhone


Features of Blue Tip Calculator:

  • Simple, easy to use design without bells or whistles
  • Support in four languages: English, German, Dutch, and Mandarin Chinese
  • Support for Apple Watch
  • Ability to split the bill with friends
  • Split screen support on iPad
  • 3D Touch Support
  • Tip calculation is done as you type

Upcoming and Planned Features:

  • An option to round to the nearest dollar
