Welcome to my website. I am a freelance iOS developer, who is obsessed with writing elegant, clean code. My passion is for learning, and I consider my day wasted if I haven't learned something new.

My most recent app is Simply Zen, a guided meditation app that is designed to be simple, calming, and best of all an inexpensive way for people to learn how to meditate. I started writing it after I got into meditation, because all of the good apps that were available on the market also cost $100 dollars per year. Simply Zen costs $5.99, and you only have to pay once. For the price of a latte, you can change your life by learning to practice meditation.

This site contains tutorials and guides for my released apps, showcases some of my accomplishments, and hosts my blog. I hope you enjoy your visit today, and hope to see you again soon!
